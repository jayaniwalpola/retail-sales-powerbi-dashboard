## Retail Sales Dashboard (Power BI)

### Tools Used
- Power BI
- SQL (MySQL)
- DAX

### KPIs
- Total Revenue
- Total Orders
- Total Customers

### Analysis Performed
- Monthly revenue trend
- Revenue by region
- Top products by revenue
- Channel performance

### Key Insights
- Online channel contributes the highest revenue
- Electronics products outperform other categories
- VIC is the strongest revenue-generating state

### Business Impact
This dashboard enables data-driven decision-making by highlighting revenue drivers, customer behavior, and regional performance.
